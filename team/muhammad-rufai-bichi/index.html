Muhammad Rufa'i Bichi

General Manager

Muhammad Rufa’i Bichi is an accomplished professional with a Bachelor’s degree in Aerospace Engineering and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) with a specialization in Agricultural Business. He currently serves as the Managing Director of RUSBI Group of Companies Limited, where he has demonstrated exceptional leadership and strategic vision in driving business growth and sustainability.

Beyond his corporate achievements, Muhammad Rufa’i Bichi is widely recognized for his commitment to community development. Through various empowerment initiatives, employment opportunities, and humanitarian support programs, he has positively impacted the lives of many. His dedication to uplifting others, particularly youth and underserved individuals, reflects his passion for social responsibility and nation-building.

A forward-thinking entrepreneur and philanthropist, Muhammad Rufa’i Bichi continues to inspire through his innovation, generosity, and unwavering commitment to economic and community advancement.
